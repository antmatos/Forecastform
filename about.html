The Story

I was bored on a snowy day and was reminiscing of the days as a kid when I'd be stuck inside playing Ruby and Sapphire. I distinctly remember wishing Castform could be real and be my little weather buddy as I went about my day to day life. I wanted to create exactly that; a little cozy buddy to sit alongside you as you work and tell you the weather! I hope you enjoy it!
